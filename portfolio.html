Biography

Mukesh Dhirubhai Ambani (born 19 April 1957) is an Indian businessman and the chairman and managing director of Reliance Industries With an estimated net worth of $123.3 billion as of July 2024, he is the richest person in Asia and 11th richest in the world. Sometimes characterized as a plutocrat, he has attracted both fame and notoriety for reports of market manipulation, political corruption, cronyism, and exploitation. Ambani's family moved back to India in 1958, where his father started a textile business. Mukesh attended the Hill Grange High School in Mumbai. He later went to the Institute of Chemical Technology (UDCT) in Matunga, Mumbai, where he earned a Bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering. He also attended Stanford University for an MBA, but he did not complete the degree, as he returned to India to help his father build Reliance. Mukesh Ambani, born on April 19, 1957, in Aden, Yemen, is an Indian billionaire and the chairman of Reliance Industries Limited (RIL). Under his leadership, RIL has become a conglomerate with interests in petrochemicals, refining, oil, telecommunications, and retail. He significantly expanded the company, establishing one of the world's largest refining complexes in Jamnagar, Gujarat, and launching Reliance Jio in 2016, revolutionizing India's telecommunications industry. Ambani, with a net worth of around $80 billion as of 2024, is one of the world's richest individuals. He is married to Nita Ambani and has three children. His philanthropic efforts through the Reliance Foundation focus on education, healthcare, and rural transformation.

Awards and Recognition

  • Padma Vibhushan: India's second-highest civilian award, received in 2016 for his contributions to trade and industry.
  • Ranked among the World's Most Powerful People by Forbes: Ambani has frequently appeared on Forbes' lists of the world's most powerful and influential people.
  • Business Awards: Numerous awards and recognitions for his business acumen and contributions to the industry, including being named the Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year.
